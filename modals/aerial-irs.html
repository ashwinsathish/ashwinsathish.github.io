Overview

A comprehensive simulation framework for aerial IRS-assisted communication systems, focusing on optimization and performance analysis through Monte Carlo methods.

Technical Implementation

  • Channel Modeling: Implemented 3D channel models for UAV-IRS scenarios
  • Optimization Framework: Developed algorithms for phase shift optimization
  • Performance Analysis: Monte Carlo simulations for various scenarios
  • Path Loss Models: Custom models for aerial communication links

Key Features

  • Dynamic UAV Positioning: Real-time optimization of UAV location
  • Phase Configuration: Efficient algorithms for IRS phase shift design
  • Coverage Analysis: Comprehensive study of coverage enhancement
  • Energy Efficiency: Analysis of power consumption and optimization

Results

  • Achieved 25% increase in coverage area
  • Reduced power consumption by 30%
  • Enhanced spectral efficiency by 40%
  • Published findings in major conferences
