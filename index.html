About Me

I am a dual degree student pursuing Int. MSc. Mathematics and B.E. Electronics & Instrumentation at BITS Pilani. My research interests lie at the intersection of wireless communications, signal processing, and applied mathematics. Currently, I am working on my Master's thesis titled 'Development and Implementation of a Simulation Concept for Multi-User RIS-aided Communication' at TU Braunschweig.

Research Interests

Wireless Communications

Exploring 5G/6G technologies with focus on network virtualization, UAV-aided communications, and optimization of wireless systems for next-generation applications.

Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces

Development of beamforming algorithms and phase optimization techniques for RIS-aided wireless networks, with emphasis on spatial awareness and environmental adaptation.

Plasmonics

Design and simulation of surface plasmon resonance sensors using novel materials and metamaterial structures for high-sensitivity biosensing applications.
